🎙 Speakers

Voices recognized across your conversations. Naming one here makes that name show up wherever the voice appears. Matching is automatic via embedding cosine similarity; tweak the threshold in Config if you see over- or under-merging.

Renaming two rows to the same name is NOT a merge

If two voices got mistakenly split into separate rows (the model thought they were different people), renaming both to Marie makes them DISPLAY as Marie but they're still two embeddings that match independently. Future captures of Marie's voice will pick one of the two and bump that row's mention count, not both.

To really fold them into one voice, tick their checkboxes below and click Merge selected. The merged row keeps the named one's name, the OR of their this is me flags, the sum of their mention counts, and a centroid embedding weighted by how often each was heard. All past transcripts that referenced the dropped rows get rewritten to point at the survivor.
